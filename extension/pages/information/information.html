TU Berlin ISIS Course Crawler
How to use?
  1. Log into the TU Berlin ISIS system
  2. Visit the main page of an ISIS course of your choice
  3. Click on the ISIS Logo in the extension bar of your browser
  4. Click "Download" and let the magic begin
Why?

Normally, a student would have to search the ISIS course website for all downloadable resources and download them all one by one. This takes a lot of time and is a very annoying, redundant and mundane task.

This browser plugin saves TU students a lot of time as they now can download all resources with ease of just one click.
